Steve Jobs

The Man who Invented Smartphones

The famous Steve Jobs staring intelligently at the camera. The co-founder and former CEO of Apple Inc.

  • Feb 24, 1955 - Steve Jobs was born in San Francisco to Joanne Carloe Schieble and Abdulfattah Jandali. The two gave up their son for adoption and Jobs was fostered to Paul and Clara Jobs.

  • 1970 - Jobs meets Steve Wozniak through a friend. This partnership becomes important to his future.

  • 1972 - Jobs graduates from Homestead High School in Cupertino, California to enrol at Reed College in Portland, Oregon. However, he drops out just a semester later.

  • 1976 - Jobs co-founds Apple Computers with Wozniaka and Ronald Wayne.

  • 1977 - Applen creates the Apple I and later on the Apple II, becoming the first comercially available PC.

  • 1980 - Apple goes public, giving Jobs a net worth of over $200 million.

  • 1985 - Jobs resigns as the chairman of Apple, founding NeXT Computers and sells $70 million of his Apple stock.

  • 1986 - Jobs buys the Graphics Group division of Lucasfilms that later becomes Pixar Animation Studios for $10 million.

  • 1995 - 1996 - Jobs becomes the CEO of Pixar, brings Pixar public right after the release of Toy Story. Later on in November 1996 he returns to Apple as an advisor as Apple buys NeXT.

  • 1997 - 2005 - Jobs becomes the CEO of Apple. And later introduces iPod after iPod, every time making it better, thinner and filled with more features.

  • 2007 - Steve Job introduces the brand new iPhone!

  • 2011 - Steve Jobs eventually has to resign as he said he would once he is unable to maintain his duties at Apple. Later than year he dies on October 5th 2011.
